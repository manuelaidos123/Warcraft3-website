The Four Factions of Warcraft III

The Alliance

The Alliance represents the forces of humanity and their closest allies. They are known for their strong military tradition and powerful magic.

  • Humans
  • Dwarves
  • High Elves
  • Gnomes

The Horde

The Horde combines the might of the Orcs and their allies. They excel in brute force and shamanic magic.

  • Orcs
  • Trolls
  • Tauren

The Scourge

The undead Scourge represents death and decay. Created by the Burning Legion, they have become a terrifying force under the Lich King.

  • Undead
  • Death Knights
  • Necromancers
