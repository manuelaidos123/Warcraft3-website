Race Strategies, Pros and Cons

Human Orc Night Elf Undead

How To Basic: Human

Human units are fragile but utilize advanced technology and magic to overcome their foes.

Advantages

  • Versatile unit composition
  • Strong defensive capabilities
  • Excellent support units
  • Powerful spellcasters

Disadvantages

  • High resource costs
  • Fragile core units
  • Slow army buildup

Key Strategies

Utilize Militia and Footmen for early expansion while teching to Rifles and Priests.

Build towers strategically while massing an army of Priests, Sorceresses, and Breakers.

How To Basic: Orc

Orcs excel at aggressive gameplay with powerful melee units and shamanistic support.

Advantages

  • Strong melee units
  • Excellent hero abilities
  • Powerful early game
  • Good mobility with Raiders

Disadvantages

  • Weak against air units
  • Limited ranged options
  • Food-heavy army composition

Key Strategies

Use Blademaster's Critical Strike and Wind Walk for early harassment while building up a Grunt force.

Utilize Raiders' Ensnare ability and speed to control enemy units while dealing damage safely.

How To Basic: Night Elf

Night Elves utilize stealth and mobility to outmaneuver opponents while leveraging powerful nature magic.

Advantages

  • Superior mobility
  • Strong economy (Moon Wells)
  • Effective harassment options
  • Powerful late game units

Disadvantages

  • Expensive units
  • Vulnerable buildings
  • Complex micro management

Key Strategies

Use Demon Hunter's survivability to secure early expansion while teching to Druids or Archers.

Combine Druids of the Claw in bear form with Druid of the Talon support for a powerful late-game army.

How To Basic: Undead

Undead excel at wearing down opponents through attrition and hero-focused strategies.

Advantages

  • Strong hero lineup
  • Efficient resource gathering
  • Good unit sustainability
  • Powerful base defenses

Disadvantages

  • Limited early game options
  • Weak tier 1 units
  • Reliant on heroes

Key Strategies

Use Death Knight's Coil and ghouls to secure an early expansion while transitioning to tier 2.

Build a core army of Crypt Fiends supported by Obsidian Statues for sustained pushes and map control.
